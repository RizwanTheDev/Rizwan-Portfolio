Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my services and process

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity. A simple website typically takes 1-2 weeks, while complex web applications or mobile apps can take 4-8 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes! I offer ongoing maintenance and support services to keep your website or app running smoothly. This includes updates, security monitoring, and technical support as needed.

What technologies do you specialize in?

I specialize in modern web technologies (HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript), Flutter for mobile development, WordPress for CMS solutions, and MySQL for databases. I stay updated with the latest industry trends and technologies.

Can you help with existing projects or only new ones?

I can work on both new projects and existing ones. Whether you need to add features, fix issues, redesign, or optimize performance, I'm happy to help improve your current digital solutions.

What information do you need to start a project?

I'll need details about your project goals, target audience, preferred features, design preferences, timeline, and budget. The more information you provide, the better I can tailor the solution to your needs.
